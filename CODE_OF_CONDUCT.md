# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge
We expect contributors to be respectful, constructive, and focused on the work.

## Expected behavior
- Be courteous in review and discussion.
- Critique ideas, not people.
- Keep feedback specific and actionable.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Deliberate disclosure of withheld material
- Malicious modification of the public preview
